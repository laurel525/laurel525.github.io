
# Circuits, plushies, and embroidary!

Laurel Barrett


## Assignment Description 

In this assignment, we started by thinking through a circuit prototype that would successfully light up three LEDs on a plushie when a switch is on and a button is pressed. After successfully testing the prototype with alligator clips, the LEDs, switch, and button are sewn onto felt with conductive thread. Extra credit is offered for embroidering a fun design on the plushie!

###### Tip for someone completing this project?

I would advise someone doing this project to go super slow doing the whip stitch around the plushie. It's easy for it to get tangled, so really take your time. Related, socks work great as stuffing -- just leave a kind of bigger gap before trying to stuff them inside!
