
# Homemade Swtiches Project!

Laurel Barrett


## Assignment Description 

This assignment focuses on crafting two homemade switches made out of conductive thread and other conductive materials. The goal is to light up an LED only when both switches are touching. I chose to create a button out of fabric from an old t-shirt and conductive fabric. I also made a switch out of a paper clip and a folding piece of conductive fabric.

###### Tip for my past self...

I would have advised myself to ask for some more conductive fabric! I didn't run out, but after I cut some apart to make my switches I realized there was a large possibility for error. If my button ended up ripping or failing in some way, I would have only had small, weird shapes of fabric left. Luckily it all worked out!
