#### Tips and Tricks for Myself

If I could go back to the beginning I would tell myself to use the smaller needle size rather than the largest. While the largest needle was the easiest to thread, my inexperience with holding the needle made it tough for me to keep the embroidery floss from falling out of the needle while I was sewing. About 3/4 of the way through the project, I switched to the smaller needle and it stayed in a lot easier!
