## Assignment Description 

In this assignment, in order to replicate the workings of our circuit plushies using C, we used nested if blocks to represent button and switch values (on or off). The value of these integers determine the value of the three LED lights and whether or not they are on (1) or off (0). The goal is to successfully emulate the circuit plushy by having all three LEDs turn on only when the switch is on and the button is pressed.
